Coco has made zines, self-published books, and released their art into the wild from time to time. Here are a few of them. 

## Books

Coco has self published two books, one of short stories and one of poetry. 

### **If Only a Cat Could Read a Book: Poetry throughout the years** 

Coco's debut poetry book shares a sweet collection of over thirty years of poems, written at all ages and for all ages to enjoy. [Buy it here](https://www.amazon.com/Only-Cat-Could-Read-Book/dp/B0BKHS2CZ5/)

### **Five Strange Short Stories**

A mix of odd tales of the supernatural and inexplicable, Coco's first self-published book appeared in 2016. [Buy it here](https://www.amazon.com/Five-Strange-Short-Stories-Elizabeth/dp/1105021505/)
